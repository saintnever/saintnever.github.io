My name is Tengxiang Zhang. I'm an electrical engineer and technology enthusiast. My interests cover a broad range including human computer interaction, pervasive computing, intent recognition, augmented human, and electromagnetics. I believe the evergrowing interaction between human and technology is a co-evolution process.

I spent my undergraduate years in Southeast University (China) from 2007 to 2011, then got my master degree from the University of Texas at Austin (US) in 2013. I worked in the US for almost two years, then went back to China due to family reasons. After designing antennas for a wearable company in Beijing for one year, I decided to pursue a higher degree. Now I'm a PhD candidate at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University.
