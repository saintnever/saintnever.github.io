Tengxiang Zhang is an electrical engineer and technology enthusiast. His interests cover a broad range, like pervasive computing, electromagnetics, smart system, IoT, wearable devices, AR, and VR. He also likes to read books and play basketball. He likes to imagine how people would interact with technologies in the future, and believes it's a co-evolution process.

He spent his undergraduate years in Southeast University (China), and went to the graduate school of the University of Texas at Austin (US) for master degree. He has worked both in US and in China. He is currently working on wearable consumer products in Beijing.
