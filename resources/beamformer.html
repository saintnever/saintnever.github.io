Beamforming


Beamformer is a spatial filter that can control the beam pattern of antenna or antenna array. It's the key enabler for space division multiple access (SDMA), which could greatly improve the throughput of wireless communication when combined with time, frequency, and code division multiple access. In this individual project, several beamforming algorithms are investigated, with the least min square (LMS) beamformer simulated to compare with maximum ratio combining (MRC) method. The simulation results show a 10 dB improvement in small scale fading margin for LMS beamformer over MRC.
