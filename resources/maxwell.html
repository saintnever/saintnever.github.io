Fish-eye


For my undergraduate senior project, I designed a half maxwell fish-eye lens antenna by using inhomogeneous metamaterial. In this project, I validated the concept in simulation, designed a IML (impedance matching layer) to improve performance, then built the antenna and measured it in anechoic chamber. It shows how metamaterials can manipulate electromagnetic waves spatially.
