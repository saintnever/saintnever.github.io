RF Module


The wireless group at Silicon Labs wanted to build a RF module that can be controlled by pure digital testers to test RF chips. I helped to review the schematic design, and was responsible for the PCB layout and hardware debugging. I also designed a calibration board and developed the whole calibration sequence using spectrum analyzer and signal generator. The module was put into production later.
