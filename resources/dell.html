Laptop Antenna Optimization

DELL Inc. would like to have a better understanding of their laptop antennas, so they initiated a preliminary study on the topic with the Wireless Network Communication Group at the University of Texas at Austin. As the graduate research assitant, I built a simplifed electromagnetic model, studied the impacts on different components, and conducted electromagnetic visibility study to optimize antenna position.
